      Experienced Compliance Regulatory Admin Opportunity

      Qualifications

      • Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience
      • 5+ years of experience in a regulatory or compliance-related role
      • Proficient in reading, interpreting, and summarizing laws and regulations at state and federal levels
      • Advanced skills in Microsoft Office applications, particularly Excel and PowerPoint
      • Strong organizational and prioritization abilities, with experience balancing multiple tasks
      • Demonstrated project management skills
      • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
      • Strong focus on teamwork, fostering trust, and collaboration
      • Proactive with a sense of urgency, able to work independently and exercise sound judgment

      Preferred Qualifications

      • Experience with legal or compliance research tools/software (e.g., NILS)
      • Paralegal or similar background

      Responsibilities

      Subject Matter Expertise:

      • Serve as the SME for regulatory compliance, specifically in health insurance related to vision services
      • Conduct federal and multi-state regulatory research and analysis

      Regulatory Documentation

      • Review and draft state/regulation-specific bulletins for dissemination
      • Ensure concise communication for organizational implementation

      Research And Analysis

      • Perform internal and external research, compile data for memos, correspondence, or information tables
      • Present condensed information for easy understanding and identification of regulatory changes

      Compliance Monitoring

      • Utilize research tools to search and alert the Compliance team about new laws, regulations, or guidelines that could impact business operations

      Join our team as a Compliance Regulatory Admin and contribute your expertise in regulatory compliance to drive organizational success. Apply now to be part of our dynamic team.
