      Vice President at Discover International

      Attention Regulatory Affairs Innovators! Join a cutting-edge biotech at the forefront of CAR-T technology as Senior Director Regulatory Affairs on a REMOTE basis.


      In this role, you will have the opportunity to drive the development and implementation of regulatory strategies that support the company's mission of improving patient outcomes using groundbreaking CAR-T technology. You'll lead cross-functional teams, engage with key stakeholders, and shape the regulatory landscape in this rapidly evolving field.


      If you are a seasoned regulatory affairs professional with a passion for innovation and a track record of success, we want to hear from you. Apply now to join a dynamic and forward-thinking team in advancing the fight against autoimmune disorders.


      • Longstanding related experience within the biotechnology/pharmaceutical industry with focus on the regulatory requirements for the drug development of Biologics/ Large Molecules
      • Proven track record in regulatory strategy planning and proven ability to plan, coordinate and lead activities
      • Knowledge of the relevant national and international regulations relating to the development and approval of biopharmaceuticals
      • Prior experience filing BLA submissions
