      Overview

      Medallia is the pioneer and market leader in Experience Management. Our award-winning SaaS platform, Medallia Experience Cloud, leads the market in the understanding and management of experience for candidates, customers, employees, patients, citizens and residents.

      We are more than a software company. We want to be known as a company that does the right thing, no matter the challenge or controversy. We are committed to creating a culture that values every person and every experience. Individual life experiences shape the way we interact with the world, which is why we encourage people to bring their whole selves to work each day. The strength of our global workforce is the most significant contributor to our success.

      We believe: Every Experience Matters. Talent is Everywhere. All Belong Here.

      At Medallia, we hire the whole person.

      Team & Role

      This leader will oversee our Employee Relations, Compliance, and HR Policy function within Medallia’s People & Culture (HR) team. The Director, Employee Relations and HR Policy will advise and assist Medallia Human Resource Business Partners and Leaders across the organization with complex employee relations investigations and consultations.

      This role is based in the US and will manage a small team. This individual will also partner cross-functionally to consult on international HR policy in the EMEA and APAC regions. This role reports directly to our EVP, Chief People Officer.

      Responsibilities


      • Program Development: Design, lead, and continuously improve programs focused on employee relations and enhancing employee morale.
      • Policy Maintenance: Maintain, recommend, and implement objectives, policies, and procedures that support the organization’s goals.
      • Investigations: Conduct thorough, timely, and impartial investigations into employee grievances and relations issues, ensuring well organized documentation and recommendations that comply with Medallia policy and applicable law.
      • Data Analysis: Rigorously track and analyze data related to employee relations to identify trends and make proactive recommendations
      • Employee Counseling: Provide counsel to employees on issues related to EEO, ADA, performance, and other key policies and guidelines.
      • Legal and Policy Consultation: Work closely with employment legal, outside counsel, and HRBPs to provide consultation on employment law and HR policy, including matters such as disciplinary proceedings, involuntary separations, sensitive performance management issues, and compliance with employment-related laws and regulations.
      • Policy Implementation: In partnership with the HRBP team, project manage the rollout of new country-specific HR policies and practices.
      • Global Projects: Participate in regional and international projects to support the business as we scale.


      Qualifications

      Minimum Qualifications


      • 7+ years experience in an Employee Relations, HR Policy, and/or HR Compliance role
      • 2+ years experience leading a team
      • An Advanced Degree
      • Prior experience with employment law, complex employment advising, and knowledge of employment best practices


      Preferred Qualifications


      • Juris Doctor Degree (JD)
      • Ability to efficiently handle and organize multiple cases at a time in a fastpaced environment
      • Able to handle confidential information in a mature and professional manner
      • Demonstrates solid judgment and experience assessing risk relative to the business need
