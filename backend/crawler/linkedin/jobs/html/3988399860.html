      Ready to bring passion into your career?


      A global leading skincare and make-up company, Clarins Group is the embodiment of a committed family history, with a fierce passion for innovation and spirit of independence. Its raison d’être is "making life more beautiful, passing on a more beautiful planet".


      Operating in more than 150 countries, we are now the number one skincare company in Europe and are accelerating our development on all other continents, particularly in Asia and the Americas.


      Because our 8,000 talents are unique, we are committed to promoting diversity in all its forms.


      Do you want to help write the next chapter of our story? We are looking for a Skincare Marketing Intern for 6-12 months based in New York City, New York. International Students welcome to apply!


      The role:

      You will provide support for the Skincare (Face) team by analyzing, reviewing and reporting on all competitive activity (advertising, products). You will also help organize and track new products, line extensions, and reformulations on an ongoing basis.


      What you’ll do:

      In an environment where everything is fast-paced, you will contribute to all of the 5 pillars of the group strategy: people-centric, brand-builder, consumer-obsessed, digitally minded, and committed to responsible beauty. Through the skincare marketing internship program, you’ll get to experience first-hand the relationship between global marketing & product development teams with the US market, the complex go-to-market strategy and execution of new products in a competitive beauty industry, the customer-centric mindset carried through in all touchpoints including collateral, in-store merchandising, and digital content creation, as well as Clarins commitment to responsible beauty that is at the heart of our brand. Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:


      • Comprehensive US market competitive analysis including presenting monthly new product launches, NPD analysis, seasonal competitive pricing deep dives, and synthesizing competitive claims, advertising, and copy associated with every new Clarins launch.
      • Assist marketing team by issuing Creative Service Requests, Project Briefs,and generating NYO requests as needed
      • Monitoring our product closet and shipping/receiving of products to retailers and from our global marketing partners
      • Assist in meeting preparation (PowerPoint presentations, conference room setup, etc)
      • During training, the candidate will learn all of the basic marketing skills necessary to promote, build and support the brand. Intern will participate in meetings, shadow marketing associates, and participate in field tours.


      You are:

      • Detail oriented with strong project management skills
      • Multi-tasker with the ability to manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment, including prioritizing and paying close attention to detail
      • Hands on with the ability to work independently
      • Customer-focused, results-oriented and brand-addicted
      • Genuinely looking forward to new challenges


      You have:

      • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Advertising, Digital Communications or related fields
      • Experience in the beauty, fashion or luxury industry, preferably in eCommerce
      • Strong communication and organizational skills
      • Proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint
      • Demonstrate strong technical acumen for troubleshooting and problem-solving


      We have:

      • Close to 70 years of expertise in beauty and spa, with the Clarins and myBlend brands
      • Unique products and innovative services
      • A commitment to Social & Environmental Responsibility embedded in our raison d'être
      • A caring and inclusive corporate culture
      • Development and training opportunities
      • A flexible work environment, with up to 3 days of remote working per week


      Salary: $18 per hour


      If you have a curious mind, a thirst to go further and the drive to boldly take on new challenges, join us.


      At Clarins Group, we believe in making life more beautiful for our employees and our customers. Inclusion and diversity are firmly grounded in our core values, which are part of our DNA. We strive to achieve inclusiveness and we foster an equal-opportunity culture where everyone can reach their full potential and do their best work. We welcome applications from all backgrounds.


      To learn more about our group and our commitments to people and the planet, visit www.groupeclarins.com
