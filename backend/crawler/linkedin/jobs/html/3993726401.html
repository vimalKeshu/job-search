      Join our team as a Compliance Payment Auditor - Apply today!

      Requirements

      • 2+ years of relevant experience
      • Intermediate Excel skills
      • Proficiency in time management
      • Ability to learn quickly
      • Experience in administrative responsibilities such as check returns
      • Knowledge of regulatory communications
      • Proficient in document auditing and tracking
      • Available to work both onsite and virtually (Hybrid role with at least 2 onsite days per week)

      Responsibilities

      Assist with Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Functions:

      • Support daily, weekly, and monthly review and audit functions
      • Document claim paid dates and notifications for audit tracking using Excel
      • Assist in pulling requested correspondence samples from vendor sites

      Custom Projects And Administrative Support

      • Complete editing and review of current strategic partner custom projects
      • Sort mail, document returned mail, and stuff letters

      We are looking for a highly motivated individual who can work independently with necessary guidance, and who is eager to learn and grow within our team. Training will be provided onsite to familiarize you with our processes and systems.

      Apply now and become a valuable member of our Compliance Payment Audit team.
