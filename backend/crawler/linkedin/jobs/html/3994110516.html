      Arthrex, Inc. is a global medical device company and a leader in new product development and medical education in orthopedics. Join our talented team at a global medical device company focused on Helping Surgeons Treat Their Patients Better™.

      Main Objective:

      Conceptualize, plan, and create effective designs for marketing and corporate initiatives. Create cutting edge, visually appealing presentations, and graphics for internal and external use, with the ability to work across multiple teams, balancing a wide range of deadlines and priorities simultaneously, while maintaining a high level of asset production. Ultimately, ensuring the final product promotes the Arthrex brand through the creation of high-quality presentations, while effectively communicating the stakeholder vision and desired tone.

      Responsibilities:

      • Creation of on-brand visual communications and business presentations for short and long-form content
      • Partner with content specialists, editors, and cross-functional clients to develop strategic and effective PowerPoint presentations for diverse, global audiences.
      • Apply brand standards to build visually engaging design assets to tell creative and effective stories through presentations.
      • Distill complex and abstract concepts to emphasize key messages with infographics, diagrams, animations, icons, graphics, charts, photography, and illustrations within presentations.
      • Manage multiple projects and priorities simultaneously; meet tight deadlines while maintaining a high quality of work, including meeting requirements and timelines while managing multiple deliverables simultaneously.
      • Keep abreast of the latest design trends and implement to brand where appropriate.
      • Facilitate revisions of existing presentation templates
      • Support internal customers with creative suggestions on how best to implement their presentations.
      • Support internal communication strategies for executive leaders

      Incidental Duties:

      The above statements describe the general nature and level of work being performed in this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all duties, and indeed additional responsibilities may be assigned, as required, by management.

      Education and Experience:

      • Bachelor’s degree required, preferably in graphic design or related field
      • 4+ years of professional presentation design experience, preferably with medical device company or agency equivalent with medical device or healthcare marketing accounts
      • Intermediate Macintosh and/or PC computer skills are essential
      • Proven ability within presentation design space

      Knowledge and Skill Requirements/Specialized Courses and/or Training:

      • Intermediate knowledge of the latest applications, styles, and layout techniques. Ability to maintain brand consistency across all projects and possess a working knowledge of layout for presentations and other forms of visual communications.
      • Intermediate knowledge of PowerPoint required, as well as a t horough working knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. InDesign preferred but not required. Motion graphics skills preferred but not required.
      • Proven ability to act as a brand advocate in communicating conceptual ideas and design rationale, all within a user-centered design process
      • Able to translate complex info into crisp narratives
      • Must thrive in high velocity and constantly evolving work environment and have excellent time management skills
      • Experienced storyboarding and high-end presentation design skills, including infographics, animation, builds, dynamic charts, etc.
      • Must be self-motivated to prioritize and manage workload and meet critical project milestones and deadlines
      • Proven problem-solving skills
      • Excellent interpersonal and collaboration skills
      • In-depth knowledge of design principles and color theory
      • In-depth knowledge of presentation development and effective oration
      • In-depth knowledge of medical device field or healthcare marketing is a plus
      • Welcomes constructive direction and use feedback to craft clear, well-organized, uncluttered presentations with optimum visual quality, messaging, and performance
      • Ability to perform in fast paced environment with tight deadlines
      • High level of accuracy and attention to detail
      • Disciplined to operate with the highest level of confidentiality and integrity
      • Self-starter with the ability to work independently

      Machine, Tools, and/or Equipment Skills:

      Intermediate Macintosh and/or PC computer skills are a must.

      Reasoning Ability:

      This position requires the ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions. Candidate needs to possess the ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.

      Mathematical Skills:

      Ability to comprehend and apply mathematical principles to the degree required to perform the job based upon job requirements in necessary for this position.

      Language and Communication Skills:

      Candidate needs to be able to comprehend and apply language skills to the degree required to perform the job based upon the job requirements listed above. Candidate must possess the ability to verbally communicate ideas and issues effectively to other team members and management.

      All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, age, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability or protected veteran status.
