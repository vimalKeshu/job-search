      About Us

      MOHELA is a nationally-recognized leader in student loan servicing and higher education financing. Established in 1981, our success for more than 40 years is due to the tremendous efforts of our outstanding staff.

      In addition to your salary, joining our team provides excellent benefits, including:

      • Generous Paid-Time-Off
      • Medical + Dental + and Vision coverage
      • 401K with dollar for dollar match up to 8% of salary after 6 months
      • Fully vested company paid pension after 5 years
      • Tuition reimbursement up to $12,000 per year after 3 months
      • Dependent child tuition reimbursement after 1 year
      • And more!

      Job Summary

      We’re seeking a dynamic leader to supervise our staff and provide a high standard of employee performance. You will guide a team through satisfying the requirements of MOHELA’s Federal and Specialty Servicing Contracts through the implementation of Authority policies and procedures and Federal Contract Compliance. Additionally, you will lead the team managing credit disputes while utilizing the e-Oscar system.

      Essential Job Functions

      • Employment - As part of the management team, assist with reviews of resumes and interviews throughout the hiring process to select the best candidates to establish a high performing customer service team. When necessary, conduct exit interviews with staff leaving the department.
      • Internal Compliance - Assure and maintain program compliance through the strategic use of MOHELA’s various systems, accuracy in interpreting each client’s program regulations and policies and the effective use of certain designated quality control standards. Oversee the development/modification of internal and external procedures for the department. Develop and maintain weekly, monthly, quarterly and other compliance reports for management as requested.
      • Department Management and Development - Monitor quantity and quality of work performed through observation and reporting and ensure work is completed timely. Develop and provide on-going training for processing tasks via internal departmental queues. Assist with establishing performance and productivity standards.
      • Departmental Function - Demonstrate strong commitment to exceptional customer service and leadership among peers. Demonstrate strong commitment to a team environment and a client focus. Demonstrate well-developed verbal and written communication skills. Ability to develop an independent viewpoint and present a compelling business case to support recommendations. Possess sound judgment and reasoning abilities. Exhibit strong drive for results and success; convey a sense of urgency to achieve outcomes and exceed expectations; persist despite obstacles, setbacks and competing influences. Ability to develop and maintain relationships with key business partners by building personal credibility and solid trust. Ability to self-motivate and self-direct. Possess strong time management and organizational skills. Ability to gain access to and cultivate strong relationships. Examine policies and procedures for servicing improvements while maintaining/decreasing servicing costs and submit possible solutions to management for approval. Create training material for internal and external customers to enhance customer service staff’s understanding. Maintain records pertaining to the volume, productivity, and quality of all workflows. Communicate effectively with all internal and external agencies contracted with MOHELA or Knowledge Finance to automate as much of the process as possible. Organize and maintain various databases such as EXCEL, ACCESS, and SharePoint to maintain and improve quality and efficiency of servicing in a third-party consultant role.
      • Analysis/ Problem Identification and Solution – Identify problems or opportunities for improvement by analyzing reporting and observing completion of servicing tasks. Gather all relevant information, organize information, and identify trends. Provide options for solutions; implementation of process improvements to increase quality and efficiency.
      • Collaboration – Build rapport with department staff and peers, interdepartmental staff and management and work together to document business requirements for process improvement initiatives, reporting, and other projects.
      • Project Planning and Management – Manage multiple projects as the project lead. Identify and document the project scope, internal and external resources needed for project tasks, timelines for implementation and if applicable, cost. Communicate progress and hurdles on projects with Executive Management.

      Minimum Requirements

      • Some college required
      • 3+ years of experience with a financial institution, secondary market, loan guarantee agency, or educational institution acting as a lender
      • 2+ years of experience in a leadership role (Combination of college education and related work experience will be considered.)
      • Ability to work onsite as a hybrid employee if living within 60 miles of a Missouri MOHELA location.

      +

      • Strong verbal and written communication skills accompanied by leadership, human relations, organizational and time management skills
      • Clear ability to work independently and to consistently and effectively complete projects with minimum supervision
      • Analytical/problem solving skills
      • Mathematical aptitude, PC experience in spreadsheets and word processing
      • Demonstrated ability to interpret financial loan data such as payment histories and credit reports while maintaining confidentiality of same

      Preferred Qualifications

      • Bachelor’s Degree
