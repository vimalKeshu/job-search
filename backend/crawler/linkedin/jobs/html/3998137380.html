      A Learning Community

      Notre Dame High School, San Jose is the center for women’s leadership in Silicon Valley and has provided an exceptional Catholic college preparatory education for young women since 1851. The school's mission is guided by the teachings of Saint Julie Billiart, the foundress of the Sisters of Notre Dame de Namur, with a strong focus on social justice to create lifelong learners, spiritual seekers, justice advocates and community leaders. Our mission, summarized by the words of Saint Julie Billiart, is to “teach them what they need to know for life.”

      The Program

      Notre Dame High School Athletics provides our student athletes with an opportunity to develop as individuals and members of the community. We offer 11 competitive interscholastic sports over three seasons and compete in the West Bay Athletic League (WBAL) and Central Coast Section (CCS).

      Responsibilities

      • Develop a vision for the athletic program that aligns with the school’s mission
      • Collaborate with athletic staff to develop scope and sequence that: recruits student-athletes, advances athletic skills and training, focuses on leadership development
      • Oversee athletic program logistics, budget, communications, and managerial tasks
      • Recruit, develop and retain skilled athletic coaches

      Education And Qualifications

      • Bachelor’s Degree required; Master’s degree preferred in sports management, health, physical education, or other related fields
      • Five years or more of management experience as an Athletics Director; coaching and teaching experience required

      Experience And Skills

      • Strong interpersonal communication
      • Ability to manage relationships with coaches, athletes, parents, faculty, media and community members
      • Resourceful, organized, detailed
      • Proven track record of leading teams and mentoring colleagues
      • Promoter of women’s education; understanding of women’s sports
      • Proactive about professional growth
