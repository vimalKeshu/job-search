      Title: Copywriter/Copy Editor

      Location: Chicago, IL

      Duration: 6 Months

      Description

      We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Copywriter/Copy Editor to produce and refine compelling content for our marketing efforts. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in writing and editing, with experience in the commercial insurance sector strongly preferred. You will play a key role in ensuring that the client’s content is clear, persuasive and aligned with our brand voice.

      Key Responsibilities

      • Content Creation: Write engaging and informative copy for various marketing materials, including website content, blog posts, email campaigns, social media, brochures, and whitepapers.
      • Editing and Proofreading: Edit and proofread content to ensure accuracy, clarity, and consistency. Correct grammatical, punctuation and spelling errors.
      • Industry Expertise: Utilize knowledge of the commercial insurance industry to create relevant and insightful content that resonates with our target audience.
      • Collaboration: Work closely with the marketing team, product managers and subject matter experts to develop content that supports our marketing strategies and business goals.
      • Research: Conduct research to stay up-to-date with industry trends and incorporate relevant information into content.
      • Brand Voice and Standards: Understand the client’s brand voice and uphold the client’s brand standards across all communications.

      Qualifications

      • Experience: Minimum of 5 years of professional copywriting and editing experience, preferably in the commercial insurance industry or a related field.
      • Education: Bachelor’s degree in English, Journalism, Marketing, Communications, or a related field.

      Skills

      • Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills.
      • Ability to translate complex insurance concepts into clear, concise, and engaging content.
      • Excellent research and analytical skills.
      • Strong attention to detail and ability to meet tight deadlines.
      • Communication: Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.

      Preferred Qualifications

      • Prior experience in the commercial insurance industry.
      • Familiarity with digital marketing strategies and tools.
      • Experience with content strategy development and execution.
