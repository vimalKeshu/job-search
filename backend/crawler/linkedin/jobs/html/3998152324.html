      • Respond Promptly: Address customer issues via phone, email, and chat in a timely manner.

      • Communication: Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

      • Organization: Stay organized, follow up effectively, and manage service ticket queues.
      • Technical Support: Provide professional hardware and software support, and escalate issues appropriately.


      ?? Experience & Qualifications:

      • Education: High School Diploma or GED.
      • Experience: At least 1 year in computer/network maintenance, desktop support, or a similar role.
      • Skills: Strong analytical abilities, organizational skills, and experience with customer support.
