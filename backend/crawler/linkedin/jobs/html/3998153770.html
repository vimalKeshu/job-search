      Health Advocates Network is currently seeking a Paralegal to work at a facility in New York, NY. These are registry positions with our company.

      Pay Rate: $33 / hour

      Shift Details

      • M-F 37.5-40 hours per week

      Job Duties

      • Assist Exec. Insurance staff in managing day to day to-do's as well as large scale initiatives
      • Plans, manages, and coordinates project activities to ensure goals and objectives of multiple and large projects are accomplished within prescribed time-frame
      • Establishes workflows and timelines and ensures project deadlines are met
      • Follow up with key stakeholders and ensuring deadlines are met in a timely manner.
      • Develops project initiation process by defining project scope, determining action items, assigning ownership, creating project timetables, and tracking execution and workflow; prepares, presents and distributes project status reports
      • Operates under general guidance and work assignments are varied and require interpretation and independent decisions on course of action
      • Performs related duties as required

      Benefits

      • Term Life
      • Short-Term Disability Coverage
      • 401K

      Qualifications

      • Bachelor's Degree required, or equivalent combination of education and related experience
      • 1-3 years of relevant experience
      • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite
      • Excellent analytical, organizational and multi-tasking abilities
      • Superior interpersonal and communication skills
      • trong work ethic and customer focus with ability to acheive results in a fast-paced, challenging environment
