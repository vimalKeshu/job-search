Enterprise Engineers build industry leading, highly intuitive software that underpin business functions across Supply Chain, Finance, Compliance, Sales, Marketing, E-commerce and Security. Our products consistently influence the enterprise software industry by defining new standards for how our customers, internally and externally, relate to their daily work. We are looking for experienced Enterprise Engineers who share a passion for tackling complexity, coaching and mentoring others, while defining the strategy and vision that guide the team to develop complex transactional systems with high availability, data integrity, and reliability.
Enterprise Engineer Responsibilities
  • Own product architecture and collaborate with product managers, designers and other engineers to set the technical direction and create software that optimizes for business metrics
  • Communicate technology and product strategy effectively to both internal and external stakeholders and leadership
  • Build industry leading enterprise systems that are highly available, intuitive, scalable, and secure
  • Produce highly reliable and performant code with appropriate monitoring and alerting
  • Analyze and improve efficiency, scalability, and stability of various system resources
  • Follow release management policies to ensure data integrity, compliance and system stability
Minimum Qualifications
  • Experience building intuitive systems in Java, PHP, C++, Python or Javascript
  • Experience in understanding code bases, including API techniques
  • Experience building and supporting production quality software
  • Experience owning projects from end-to-end
Preferred Qualifications
  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • Experience working with code bases building REST APIs, GraphQL and using service oriented architecture (SOA)
  • Experience modeling data flows between systems to enable transactional workflows and upstream/downstream integrations
  • Experience building secure software with auditable access controls and permissions
  • SQL experience in handling volumes of data, performance tuning
