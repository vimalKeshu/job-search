Enterprise Engineer

Location pin icon
Fremont, CA
Apply to Job
Enterprise Engineering is at the heart of Meta's mission. By transforming the way businesses function, we are helping to bring billions of people around the world closer together. This requires unconventional thinking and lightning-fast, nimble solutions. We build enterprise products for Meta first. The Infra Operations Products & Services (IOPS) team builds, operates, and provides best-in-class operational products and services for monitoring, troubleshooting, and remediation of Meta's infrastructure. We are looking for engineers who share a passion for tackling and developing complex systems with high scalability, availability, data integrity, and reliability.
Enterprise Engineer Responsibilities
  • Build industry leading enterprise systems that are highly available, intuitive, scalable, and secure
  • Work with product managers, designers and other engineers to create software that optimizes for business metrics
  • Produce highly reliable and performant code with appropriate monitoring and alerting
  • Conduct design and code reviews to ensure scalability, performance, and alignment with standards and best practices
  • Follow release management policies to ensure data integrity, compliance and system stability
Minimum Qualifications
  • 5+ years of experience building intuitive systems in Java, PHP, C++ or JavaScript technologies such as ReactJS or AngularJS
  • 5+ years experience working with code bases building micro services, REST APIs, GraphQL
  • 5+ years experience modelling data flows between systems to enable transactional workflows and upstream/downstream integrations
  • 5+ years experience building scalable, distributed systems and products
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, relevant technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience building and supporting production quality software
  • Experience with GenAI use cases
  • Experience with data pipelines and troubleshooting data pipelines

About Meta
Meta builds technologies that help people connect, find communities, and grow businesses. When Facebook launched in 2004, it changed the way people connect. Apps like Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp further empowered billions around the world. Now, Meta is moving beyond 2D screens toward immersive experiences like augmented and virtual reality to help build the next evolution in social technology. People who choose to build their careers by building with us at Meta help shape a future that will take us beyond what digital connection makes possible today—beyond the constraints of screens, the limits of distance, and even the rules of physics.
