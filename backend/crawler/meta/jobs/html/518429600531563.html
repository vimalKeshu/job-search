Solutions Architect

Location pin icon
London, UK
Apply to Job
Our team is looking for a high performance Solutions Architect with an entrepreneurial mindset. As a Solutions Architect, you will lead the advertising industry by enabling clients to realize the full potential of Meta's marketing solutions and even defining new solutions. You will work with marketing executives at top-tier companies on their industry-leading advertising initiatives, translating their business goals into an execution plan using Meta’s suite of cutting-edge advertising platform. This role will allow you to apply your passion for problem solving to achieving real-world marketing results at Meta scale.
Solutions Architect Responsibilities
  • Act as a technical consultant and subject matter expert on Meta's marketing technology solutions for the marketing organizations at some of the most innovative companies in the world
  • Guide our clients to the most optimal implementations by walking them through the technical details Meta's advertising platform and products
  • Work closely with sales teams to unlock and develop new opportunities by developing a deep understanding on business goals and industry trends
  • Influence the marketing industry through technology by participating in industry conferences and presenting to executive-level audience
  • Understand and apply knowledge of products, technologies and business to build solutions to solve for problems at scale
  • Independently break down coding projects into tasks and partner with all applicable teams and partners to meet pre-established goals
  • Build relationships with team members and cross functional partners
  • document and share project updates and insights
  • influence decision-making through presentation of data-centric business topics
  • Participate in interviewing and on-boarding of new team members
  • Willingness to travel 25% of the time
Minimum Qualifications
  • Experience with web, mobile, or enterprise technology stack
  • Experience communicating with technical and business audience at prospects or clients
  • Experience demonstrating knowledge of industry technology areas and trends
  • Technical Degree or equivalent experience
  • Eexperience working with software systems including querying, SQL scripting, and working with API development
  • Combined experience as an Engineer, Solutions Architect, Technical Consultant, or Partner/Sales Engineer
Preferred Qualifications
  • Technical understanding of Meta advertising products (including Ads and Pages API, Identity Platform)
  • Experience deploying large partner integration projects
  • Experience working with API documents, calling API’s using scripts, and testing API’s
  • Experience with Swagger and Postman
  • Knowledge of AdTech, Advertising, or Digital Marketing (Brand or Direct Response)

About Meta
Meta builds technologies that help people connect, find communities, and grow businesses. When Facebook launched in 2004, it changed the way people connect. Apps like Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp further empowered billions around the world. Now, Meta is moving beyond 2D screens toward immersive experiences like augmented and virtual reality to help build the next evolution in social technology. People who choose to build their careers by building with us at Meta help shape a future that will take us beyond what digital connection makes possible today—beyond the constraints of screens, the limits of distance, and even the rules of physics.
