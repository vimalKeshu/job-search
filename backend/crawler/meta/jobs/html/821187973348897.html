Meta is looking for a driven, results oriented manager to join our Governance Team. The team's mission is to partner with teams across Meta to design, build, and manage decision making mechanisms and models of governance that give people greater power over Meta’s products and decisions. We are the team that established the Oversight Board to help govern Meta’s approach to content moderation and have more recently established Community Forums as a way to democratize input into our innovation. In this role, you will be responsible for supporting the continued development of Community Forums and other governance innovations, with an emphasis on driving the substantive design to maximize the value and impact to Meta’s products and policies. To do this, you will partner closely with teams such as Product, Policy, and Legal. The ideal candidate will have strong knowledge and experience working with Product teams as well as being a self-starter, comfortable with ambiguity, detail oriented in their work, and possessing a commitment toward continued evaluation and refinement of business processes. The candidate should also have excellent program management and communication skills, enabling them to work with key business stakeholders to understand project requirements, lead on the management and execution of workstreams, and represent Meta in communications to internal and external stakeholders at all levels. You should be passionate about the services we provide and the people we serve.
Governance Manager Responsibilities
  • Lead the substantive design of Meta’s Community Forums and other governance innovations to maximize value and impact on Meta’s products and policies.
  • Partner with cross-functional teams such as Product, Policy, and Legal in respect of such initiatives.
  • Help identify and resolve issues related to Community Forums.
  • Lead or support upon key team projects and ad hoc governance initiatives.
  • Cultivate culture of collaboration and executional excellence.
Minimum Qualifications
  • 6+ years of experience in product, or product-adjacent work including user research, ops, content or product policy, or product legal.
  • Experience in or understanding of product development, design, and research.
  • Experience in project design and management and playing a lead program or project management role.
  • Effective communication skills, as well as demonstrated experience of problem solving in a complex, multi-faceted environment involving multiple stakeholders.
  • Creative problem-solving and multi-tasking skills in time-sensitive settings.
  • Bachelor’s degree
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience in external stakeholder engagement.
  • Experience working directly with senior executives and stakeholders.
